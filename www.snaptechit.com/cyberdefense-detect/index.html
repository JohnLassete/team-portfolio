MDR Solutions circumvent potential attack, preserving a client’s financial & operational integrity

— Fast food restaurant chain, Southeastern United States

With Snap Tech IT’s MDR Solution in place, the 24/7/365 monitoring detected a login breach. Someone tried to log in on an IP address in one city and an hour later, the very same login was detected in another city. Without enough time for a user to physically travel from the first city to the second city, our Security Operations Center (SOC) alerted the client of the potential attack & requested that they change passwords immediately to prevent infiltration.

MDR also detected the anatomy of the potential attack, enabling our Security Operations Center (SOC) to prevent similar threats in the future.

Just another day in the life of Snap Tech IT CyberDefense!
